Our Story

Over 500,000 people in the US experienced homelessness in 2017, including 23,000 in Cleveland alone. Many of these people are lacking basic necessities like toiletries.
Mentor Pack offers curated personal care subscription boxes. When you suscribe to a box, the proceeds go toward providing backpacks filled with personal care items to Cleveland homeless shelters.
The best part? By subscribing to our box, you also have the opportunity to join our Pack and provide mentorship to someone experiencing homelessness in the Cleveland area.
